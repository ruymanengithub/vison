Builds CHINJ01 script structure dictionary.

Parameters:
  • IDL – int, [mV], value of IDL (Inject. Drain Low).

  • IG1s – list of 2 ints, [mV], [min,max] values of IG1.
  • id_delays – list of 2 ints, [mV], injection drain delays (2).

  • diffvalues – dict, opt, differential values.
